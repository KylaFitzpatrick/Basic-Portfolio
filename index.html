About Me

My name is Kyla Fitzpatrick. I was born in San Francisco, CA on March 26, 1991. I grew up in a small little town called Mill Valley located north of San Francisco. I lived in Mill Valley until I started college at Sonoma State Univeristy. I decided I wasnted to explore the world outside of California and decided to live with my grandpa in Northern Virgina. I attended community college there for about a year. Then I transferred to University of North Carolina at Carlotte. During my time there, I studied abroad in Barcelona and graduated with a BSBA in International Business.

Shortly after graduating I landed an internship as a release cordinator at a hedge fund company. After my internship ended I was hired back as a junior QA Analyst. Since then I moved to the Bay Area then lived in Austin, Texas for 3 years before deciding to join The Boot Camp at UT Austin. My hobbies include spending time with family and friends. I enjoy traveling, camping and listening to all kinds of music, but my favorite genre is indie. I love attending concerts and festivals especially Austin City Limits.
